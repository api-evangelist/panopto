Higher education faculty face the tension of designing for authentic learning when students can produce polished written responses with AI in seconds. The post outlines six video-based teaching strategies, drawing on insights from Dr. Derek Bruff (UVA Center for Teaching Excellence) and Panopto Chief Customer Officer Ian Paice, arguing that video captures the learning process rather than just the final product, making AI misuse harder to conceal.
